Simple Omelette Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them with a fork until well mixed. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Heat the butter or oil in a non-stick frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan and tilt the pan to spread them evenly.
  4. As the eggs begin to set, use a spatula to gently push the cooked parts from the edges toward the center, allowing uncooked eggs to flow to the edges.
  5. When the eggs are mostly set but still slightly runny on top, add any fillings if desired.
  6. Fold the omelette in half and cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate and serve immediately.
